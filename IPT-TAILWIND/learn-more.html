USE RULES
  1. Do not bring your gym bag onto the fitness floor, we have provided lockers and open shelves for your belongings.
  2. WEAR CLEAN CLOTHES while working out. Inappropriate body odor is offensive. Dry, closed-top athletic shoes are required. No dress shoes, boots or sandals are permitted.
  3. Refrain from yelling, using profanity, banging weights and making loud sounds. Malicious, perverted and violent acts are strictly prohibited; anyone caught or reported shall be escorted out of the premises.
  4. Re-rack weights and return all other equipment and accessories to their proper locations.
  5. Ask staff to show you how to operate equipment properly so that others are not waiting as you figure it out.
  6. Stick to posted time limits on all cardiovascular machines.
  7. Do not bring children onto the gym floor. Children must remain in the waiting area.
  8. Do not disturb others. Focus on your own workout and allow others to do the same.
  9. Before beginning your workout, wash your hands and wipe off any cologne or perfume.
  10. Intoxicated and/or drugged clients/visitors will be asked to leave the premises.
  11. Herdoza Fitness Center shall not be liable for any lost or damaged belongings.
INDIVIDUALS NOT ADHERING TO THESE POLICIES MAY BE ASKED TO LEAVE THE FACILITY.
